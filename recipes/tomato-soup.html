Tomato Soup

The perfect recipe

A yummy tomato soup, goes well with a nice slice of bread!

Ingredients

  • 4 Cups chopped fresh tomatoes
  • 2 Cups chicken broth
  • 4 Cloves garlic
  • 1 Large slice of onion
  • 2 Tablespoons butter
  • 2 Tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 Teaspoons white sugar, or to taste
  • 1 Teaspoon salt, or to taste

How to make

  1. Add the tomatoes, chicken broth, garlic and a large slice of onion in the stockpot over a medium heat. Bring it to a boil and allow to simmer for 20 minutes.
  2. Remove from the heat and run the simmered mixture through a blender. Once blended move the mixture to a large bowl.
  3. Melt the butter over a medium heat in the empty stock pot. Stir in the flour to make a roux by cooking and whisking constantly until the mixture turns medium brown.
  4. Gradually pour in the tomato mixture and whisk as you go to prevent lumps forming. Then stir in the rest.
  5. Season with sugar and salt to taste.
  6. If it has cooled down, warm it back up in a pan and serve it hot.
